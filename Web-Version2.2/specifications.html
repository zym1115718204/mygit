FengSensor

Focus on Indoor Environment Quality

FengSensor is used for indoor environmental quality monitoring . It is equipped with mobile phone Apps and web server . FengSensor provides real-time and historical monitoring for your indoor environment quality , including carbon dioxide concentration , PM2.5 concentration , formaldehyde concentration , temperature , humidity , illumination and noise level , etc .

Instructions and Notes

Placement way：Vertical or horizontal

Notes

Please do not place the device in humid environments such as bathrooms , toilets, etc. for a long time.
Please keep the device out of the reach of children and pets.
Please keep the device away from open flames.
